The Castlegate Classic, June 29th, 2019

Announcing the next named Hyper event in Marietta, GA

Written by Brendan Hagan on May 27, 2019
Tags: announcement events

Where: Giga Bites Cafe, Marietta, GA
When: 06/29/2019, 2pm ET
Who: Hopefully everybody
Cost: $10
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/events/2955608484479385/
Stream: https://twitch.tv/tuskvision

The Castlegate Hotel and Conference Center was instrumental in the early days of Magic in Atlanta, Georgia, hosting numerous conventions, including the first Dragoncon, as well as early Prereleases. For more information on the history of the Castlegate, please read the excellent 2008 Atlanta Fantasy Fair writeup.

Location Information:

Giga-Bites Cafe
1851 Roswell Rd
Marietta, GA 30062
